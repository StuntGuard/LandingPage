About us

StuntGuard, an innovative application to ensure and detect Stunting on children. Did you know? Stunting or stunted growth in children refers to a condition where a child's height is significantly below the average for their age and gender caused by lack of nutrition. In 2022, Indonesia's stunting prevalence value reached 21.6% by Indonesian Nutrition Status Survei (SSGI) report from ministry of health, it make us to take action and idea in order to prevent it become more worse. To tackle it, we create an application that will help the parent to be more aware with stunting and provide them with any necessary information that will help them to prevent it on their child.

Our Feature

We always come up with useful features inside our apps

Weight Factor

Added feature to include child's weight as an additional factor in statistical predictions. This is important because body weight is a crucial health indicator in children. By entering this information, the application can provide more accurate and relevant predictions of the child's health condition.

Periodic Notification Feature

Added periodic notification feature for assigned missions and to check child health status. Regular notifications help parents stay connected with their child's progress. This ensures that they always have the latest information and can act in a timely manner if necessary, increasing overall app usage.

Smart Food Recipe

Personalized, nutritious meal suggestions using machine learning to combat stunting, simplify meal planning, save time, and improve user engagement by continuously learning from user interactions.
